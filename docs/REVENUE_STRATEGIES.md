- Revenue share: 50% creator / 50% platform

**3. User-Generated Content**
- Free tier for community contributions
- Premium tier for professional submissions
- Revenue share on premium content

**4. In-House Content Creation**
- Hire motion graphics artists
- Create exclusive content
- Differentiator from stock sites

**5. AI-Generated Content**

- Generate unique variations
- Lower production costs
